As part of its public awareness mission, AIA Cyber Risk Solutions created CyberNest Digital Safety, a free digital safety app designed to help individuals and families build stronger cybersecurity habits. CyberNest provides simple lessons, practical checklists, trusted resources, and guided reminders covering topics such as account protection, password safety, phishing awareness, phone scams, device security, home Wi-Fi, privacy, backups, and family online safety.

CyberNest reflects the company’s belief that cybersecurity should not only be reserved for large organizations or technical experts. Everyone deserves access to clear, trustworthy guidance that helps them protect their digital life. By offering CyberNest as a free public resource, AIA Cyber Risk Solutions demonstrates its commitment to improving cybersecurity awareness beyond traditional consulting services.

At its core, AIA Cyber Risk Solutions exists to make cybersecurity more understandable, actionable, and effective. Whether supporting an organization with risk and vulnerability management or helping families recognize common online threats, the company’s mission is to turn cyber risk into informed action.
